## App Store Description

**Kaiwa** is a real-time conversation translator for iOS. Built for the moments when you and the person in front of you don't share a language — and you need to actually talk, not fumble with a translation app.

**How it works**

Start a session. Set the phone between you. Speak naturally. Kaiwa transcribes what each person says and translates it instantly — Japanese and English, side by side, each facing the right person.

No typing. No copying and pasting. No switching modes. Just conversation.

**Who it's for**

- Travelers in Japan navigating real-life situations
- Language learners practicing with native speakers
- Families with members who speak different languages
- Anyone who's ever wished there was an easier way

**Features**

- Live transcription and translation during conversation
- Two-sided layout — each person reads text facing them
- Automatic language detection (Japanese and English)
- Shows original text with translation beneath it
- Screen stays on during active sessions
- Runs in portrait — no accidental rotations mid-conversation

Kaiwa is currently in early access. New accounts require approval. If you sign up and your account is pending, please get in touch.
