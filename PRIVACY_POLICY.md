# Privacy Policy — Kaiwa

*Last updated: March 4, 2026*

Kaiwa ("the app", "we", "us") is a real-time conversation translation app for iOS. This policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

---

## What We Collect

### Account Information
When you sign in, we collect your **email address** and any linked authentication identifiers (Apple ID, Google account, etc.) via Clerk, our authentication provider. This is used solely to identify your account and manage access.

### Microphone Audio
Kaiwa captures microphone audio **during active sessions only** — when you explicitly start a session by tapping the play button. Audio is streamed to Soniox for real-time transcription and is **not stored** by us or Soniox after processing. We do not record or retain audio.

### Translation Content
Text produced by transcription is translated via our Convex backend. Transcribed and translated text is **not persisted** on our servers. It exists only in memory during your session and is discarded when you end the session.

### Session Metadata
We store minimal session metadata (timestamps, session counts) to enforce fair-use limits and manage subscription access. This data is not shared with third parties.

---

## What We Do Not Collect

- Precise or approximate location
- Contacts or address book
- Browsing history or cross-app tracking
- Health or financial data
- Any data while the app is in the background

## Data Retention

- **Account data**: retained while your account is active. Deleted on request.
- **Audio**: never stored; processed and discarded in real time.
- **Session metadata**: retained for as long as your account is active.

---

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

---

## Children

Kaiwa is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
